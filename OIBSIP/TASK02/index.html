Sundar Pichai

CEO of Alphabet Inc. and Google

Introduction

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive, born on July 12, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. He is the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc., the parent company of Google.

Leadership at Google

Pichai joined Google in 2004 and played a pivotal role in the development of Google Chrome, Chrome OS, and other Google products. He became the CEO of Google in 2015 and later assumed the role of CEO of Alphabet Inc. in 2019.

Accomplishments

Under Sundar Pichai's leadership, Google has continued to be a global technology leader, expanding its products and services across various domains, including search, advertising, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence.

Legacy

Pichai is widely recognized for his strategic vision, leadership, and contributions to the tech industry. His dedication to innovation and commitment to excellence have solidified his position as a key figure in the world of technology.
